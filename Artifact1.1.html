Artifact 1.1:
Clarinet Alternate Fingerings

Course: Introduction to Digital Media

Description

This individual project is a website that contains the image, audio, and video projects from the course. The subject is clarinet playing.

How it Represents Competency 1.1 (Design & Development of Learning Systems)

The media files focus on aesthetics and provide performance support to young clarinet players in the form of additional materials outside their method book.

Reflection

Even though I have graphic design experience, I took this course to improve my video production skills. I learned that, even though the media designed may be aesthetically pleasing, it still has to support the performance objectives. Storyboarding played a great role in helping me to create a video that is (hopefully) engaging and supports the performance objectives. I still have a way to go and will continue to learn more about video production and editing.

Artifact Information

The website contains:

  • A digital image with the techniques used, design elements, and reflection
  • A video with a storyboard, techniques, and reflection
  • Audio with techniques and reflection
  • Link to Articulate 360 quiz
  • Appropriate copyright information for all materials
