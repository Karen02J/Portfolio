Usability Artifact 1.2:
Survey Design Tools

Course: Interaction Design

Description

This artifact is a PDF report comparing two survey design tools using interaction and interface guidelines to determine which one works best for me. Also included is the form I created using one of the platforms (Jotform) and the design principles I applied to the form.

How it Represents Usability Competency 1.2 (Design Frameworks & Theories)

I used usability and interactivity principles within the requirements of the assignment to select a tool and design a form.

Reflection

Who knew that creating a form would require so much thought and effort? When I really thought about this, I realized that I had filled out forms that are quite frustrating. For example, they contained no error messages to tell you why your form failed to submit. The interaction principles I learned here helped me to understand why I may have been frustrated with such forms. I used the information from this course to learn how to create fillable PDFs to use at work.
