Usability Artifact 1.4:
Trash Warrior Trash App

Course: Interaction Design

Description

This is a report contains all of the documentation for the prototype for a trash removal app. I was in a team of three people and wrote 1/3 of the report, designed the logo, contributed two mock ups, coordinated and facilitated Zoom meetings, and managed the document presentation and turn-in of the report.

How it Represents Usability Competency 1.4

The report contains descripts of the high fidelity and the low fidelity of prototypes with screen captures representing them. The link to the prototype is no longer available. This PDF documents the process from determining the audience, to testing, to the high fidelity prototype.

Reflection

I never realized that there is so much work involved in designing an app. First, there has to be a purpose for an app. Then, it has to be designed in such a way that it meets the approval of the stakeholders and the needs of the end user. The documentation of this process seemed tedious and unnecessary at times, but in the end, the information proved to be valuable and would really be useful in the launch of an app.

Artifact Information
