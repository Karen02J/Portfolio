Designing Online Learning
Spring 2018

Course Description

Instructor: Dr. David Reid

Students will practice designing online lessons for meaningful learning outcomes such as knowledge construction, building models, knowledge transmission, and community building and working collaboratively.

Course Competencies

  • Practice designing online lessons within the framework of an entire online course for meaningful learning outcomes such as knowledge construction, building models, knowledge transmission, and community building and working collaboratively.
  • Learn to locate, evaluate and use Internet based tools in your online courses/lessons to enhance the capabilities of a Learning Management System.
  • Develop meaningful assessments for online lessons.
  • Learn about and practice strategies associated with the unique issues of implementing an online course (e.g. aligning course objectives to assessments and activities, choosing learning activities suitable for online learning, and assessing higher-order thinking skills).
