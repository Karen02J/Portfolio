Teaching Online
Summer 2020

Course Description

Instructor: Dr. Jane Howland

Learn to be an effective online instructor! Examine issues in teaching and learning online; instructor and student roles; instructional strategies for supporting diverse learners; methods of student assessment; online communication; classroom management; characteristics of online learning management systems

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify critical components of social presence and issues inherent in development of an online learning community.
  • Use asynchronous and synchronous methods to design small group and whole class discussions.
  • Identify effective methods of communicating with students and families.
  • Select appropriate technologies to support online learning.
  • Design, develop, implement, and evaluate an online learning activity that includes student assessment.
  • Create and participate in instructor-selected and self-selected small groups.
  • Evaluate your knowledge and competency as an online instructor.
