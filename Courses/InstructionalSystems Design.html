Instructional Systems Design
Spring 2017

Course Description

Instructor: Dr. Jane Howland

Develop knowledge and skills related to the systematic design of instruction. Learn to analyze, design, develop, implement, and evaluate learning systems and instruction. Identify appropriate technologies to support learning and explore alternative models of instructional design.

Learning Outcomes

  • Apply elements of the ADDIE model (analyze, design, develop, implement, and evaluate).
  • Elect appropriate technologies to support learning.
  • Design adult learning experiences using the Backward Design/ADDIE model.
  • Analyze emerging models of ISD and explain why they are useful.
